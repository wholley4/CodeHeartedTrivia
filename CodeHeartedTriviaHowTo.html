How To

Code Hearted Trivia is a computer science based question and answer game.

Rules:

First, choose a topic.

Next, simply answer the question. If you answer correctly, the amount the question was worth is added to your overall prize total. If not, your prize total does not change

The player is allowed to choose one helper from the helper screen. This is the helper that will give you a hint if you decide to use up your hint.

Only one hint is allowed per game.

The prize tier is how much the question is worth. As you advance through the game, the value of the questions will increase.
