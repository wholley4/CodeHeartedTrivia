How To

Code Hearted Trivia is a computer science based question and answer game.

Rules:

First, pick a helper. Each helper has their own area of expertise that they can assist you on.

After, at the gameboard, pick a question. There are 10 question, each from a different topic, ranging from Freshman to Graduate Student knowledge.

Put in your answer. For every correct answer, the amount in the prize tower iwill increase.

The game ends when all questions have been answered.
